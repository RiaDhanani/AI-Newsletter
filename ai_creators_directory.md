# AI Creators Directory
*A curated list of 28 creators who post high-signal AI content daily or very regularly, organized by content type and platform.*

---

## How to Use This File in Cowork
- Drop this file into your Cowork project folder
- Add a project instruction like: *"Reference ai_creators_directory.md when looking for Creator Spotlight candidates or sourcing AI news"*
- Ask Cowork to filter by platform, content type, or recency (e.g. "Who from this list posts daily on Instagram?" or "Find the latest post from Simon Willison")

---

## Content Types
- **Research** — ML papers, model releases, technical deep dives
- **News & Tools** — Daily AI tool roundups, product launches, trending news
- **Education** — Tutorials, courses, concept explainers
- **Dev & Code** — Open-source projects, engineering, hands-on builds
- **Business & Work** — AI strategy, adoption, careers, real-world use cases

---

## Creators

### Andrej Karpathy
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@karpathy), YouTube, GitHub
- **Focus:** Deep learning fundamentals, LLMs from scratch, neural net intuition. Former Tesla AI Director and OpenAI co-founder. Posts infrequently but always high-signal.
- **Best for:** Understanding the "why" behind modern AI architectures

---

### Simon Willison
- **Type:** Dev & Code
- **Platforms:** X (@simonw), GitHub (github.com/simonw), simonwillison.net
- **Focus:** Daily LLM experiments, tooling breakdowns, practical prompting. Builds open-source CLI tools including `llm` and Datasette. Posts something new almost every single day.
- **Best for:** Practical, daily AI engineering updates

---

### Sebastian Raschka
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@rasbt), Substack (Ahead of AI), GitHub
- **Focus:** Deep dives into LLM architectures, LoRA, fine-tuning, and model evaluation. "Ahead of AI" is one of the most technically rigorous AI newsletters available.
- **Best for:** Technical LLM research without hype

---

### Ethan Mollick
- **Type:** Business & Work
- **Platforms:** X (@emollick), Substack (One Useful Thing), LinkedIn
- **Focus:** AI in work, education, and research. Wharton professor. Most-recommended free AI newsletter. Grounded, practical, consistently free of hype.
- **Best for:** Applying AI in real professional contexts

---

### Matt Wolfe
- **Type:** News & Tools
- **Platforms:** X (@mreflow), YouTube
- **Focus:** Daily AI tool roundups, new product launches, weekly news summaries. One of the best sources for knowing what shipped this week.
- **Best for:** Staying current on new AI tools and launches

---

### Yannic Kilcher
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@ykilcher), YouTube
- **Focus:** Breaks down ML research papers with wit and skepticism. Good for evaluating whether new models are actually useful or just hype.
- **Best for:** Critical analysis of new AI research papers

---

### Grant Sanderson (3Blue1Brown)
- **Type:** Education
- **Platforms:** YouTube (@3blue1brown)
- **Focus:** Mathematics and neural networks through animations. Best visual introduction to how transformers and deep learning actually work.
- **Best for:** Visual, intuitive understanding of AI math and architecture

---

### Andrew Ng
- **Type:** Education
- **Platforms:** X (@AndrewYNg), Substack (The Batch), LinkedIn, YouTube
- **Focus:** Weekly overview of the most important AI news with editorial commentary. Co-founder of Google Brain, Coursera, and DeepLearning.AI.
- **Best for:** Weekly structured AI overview from a leading expert

---

### Allie K. Miller
- **Type:** Business & Work
- **Platforms:** X (@alliekmiller), LinkedIn
- **Focus:** AI business strategy, Fortune 500 AI adoption, careers in AI. Most-followed AI business expert on LinkedIn with 2M+ followers. Former Amazon and IBM.
- **Best for:** AI strategy and business adoption

---

### DAIR.AI
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@dair_ai), Substack, GitHub
- **Focus:** Paper threads, research explainers, weekly ML paper roundups. Known for the "ML Papers of the Week" series.
- **Best for:** Curated ML research summaries

---

### Jack Clark

- **Focus:** "Import AI" newsletter -- deep coverage of AI safety and research. Co-founder of Anthropic, former Policy Director at OpenAI.
- **Best for:** AI safety and frontier research coverage

### Hugging Face

### Zain Kahn (Superhuman AI)

- **Focus:** Daily AI tool tips, ready-to-use prompts, and practical tutorials. Superhuman AI has over 1 million subscribers.

### Cameron Wolfe
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@cwolferesearch), Substack (Deep Learning Focus)
- **Focus:** Contextualizes and explains important AI research topics. "Deep Learning Focus" consistently cited as one of the best technical deep learning newsletters.
- **Best for:** Research explainers with technical depth

### The Gradient / Daniel Bashir
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** Substack (thegradient.pub), YouTube
- **Focus:** Articles and podcast interviews with AI researchers. Covers technical, social, and economic angles of AI.
- **Best for:** Long-form AI research journalism and interviews

---

### Latent Space
- **Type:** Dev & Code
- **Platforms:** X (@latentspacepod), Substack
- **Focus:** AI Engineer newsletter and top technical AI podcast. Covers how leading labs build agents, models, and infrastructure.
- **Best for:** AI engineering and infrastructure, podcast format

---

### AI Explained
- **Type:** News & Tools
- **Platforms:** YouTube (@aiexplained-official), X
- **Focus:** Breaks down cutting-edge AI research and news -- LLMs, AGI debates, OpenAI and Anthropic releases -- into clear, watchable episodes.
- **Best for:** Video-format AI news and research breakdowns

---

### Greg Brockman
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@gdb)
- **Focus:** OpenAI co-founder and chairman. Posts updates on OpenAI research, vision, and challenges.
- **Best for:** Inside perspective on frontier AI development

---

### Demis Hassabis
- **Type:** Research
- **Platforms:** X (@demishassabis)
- **Focus:** CEO of Google DeepMind. Posts on frontier AI research, AlphaFold, AI for science, and the long-term vision for AGI.
- **Best for:** AI for science and frontier research perspective

---

### Nandini Mullaji
- **Type:** Business & Work
- **Platforms:** LinkedIn (@nandinimullaji), X
- **Focus:** AI in product, business applications, and practical use cases. Posts consistently on real-world AI adoption and strategy.
- **Best for:** Daily business-focused AI content

---

### AI Skill of the Week
- **Type:** Education
- **Platforms:** Substack
- **Focus:** Weekly newsletter teaching one practical, actionable AI skill per issue -- prompting, workflow automation, tool walkthroughs, and hands-on use cases for professionals.
- **Best for:** Weekly practical skill-building for non-technical professionals

---

### Arnav Jaitly
- **Type:** Dev & Code
- **Platforms:** LinkedIn (arnavjaitly), X (@arnitly), Instagram (@arnitly)
- **Focus:** Data Scientist and NLP engineer sharing hands-on AI builds, LLM fine-tuning techniques (LoRA, RLHF, DPO), and practical AI engineering insights. Agentic workflows, KYC AI agents, NER models.
- **Best for:** Practical NLP engineering and LLM fine-tuning

---

### Evolving AI
- **Type:** News & Tools
- **Platforms:** Instagram (@evolving.ai)
- **Handle:** @evolving.ai
- **Followers:** 4 million
- **Focus:** High-volume daily AI news, tool launches, and trending AI content formatted for Instagram. Bills itself as the No.1 AI community on Instagram. 2,481+ posts. Good source for what is going viral in AI on social media and for quick-hit tool and product announcements that have not yet reached the newsletter press.
- **Best for:** Trending AI content on Instagram, early signal on viral AI tools and news
- **Note for Cowork:** Use as a supplementary signal source for Quick Shots and Tool Drop. When a tool or story appears on @evolving.ai before it appears in the press, treat it as an early indicator and verify through a primary source before including in the newsletter.
